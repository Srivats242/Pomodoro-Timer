Pomodoro Timer

A Pomodoro Timer is a tool that uses the Pomodoro Technique, a time management technique that helps people manage their time and help improve focus and productivity. It marks off 25-minute work intervals, known as pomodoros that are seperated by 5-minute breaks. The Pomodoro Technique helps you concentrate on different tasks for fixed intervals and after four pomodoros, you can take a longer break of 15-30 minutes.
